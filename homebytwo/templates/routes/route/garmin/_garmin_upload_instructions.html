Step-by-step instructions

Before your activity

  1. Upload the route to Garmin Connect
  2. Once the route has been uploaded, follow the link to the activity page
  3. On the activity page, choose "Send To Device"
  4. Sync your device

When you start your activity

  1. Select the Activity type and go to the activity options
  2. Choose "Race an Activity" and then "Downloaded"
  3. Start and have fun!
