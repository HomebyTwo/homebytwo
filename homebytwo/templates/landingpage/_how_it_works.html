How does it work?

  • We calculate your individual performance profile based on your Strava history. The performance profile is an estimation of how fast you move in relation to the steepness of the terrain.
  • You can import any route from Strava or Switzerland mobility. For each route, Homebytwo proposes checkpoints based on places found along the course. You can choose which ones to include in your schedule.
  • The estimated schedule is based on your performance profile. During the activity, you see how you are doing against your plan at every checkpoint.
  • Tell Homebytwo when you started the activity and it will tell you how you are comparing to your schedule based on where you are. No need for an app in the background.
