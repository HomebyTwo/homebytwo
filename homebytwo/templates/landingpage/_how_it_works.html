How it works

  • Before the activity, we look at your Strava account and calculate an individual performance profile for each type of activity.
  • When you import a route we calculate the estimated schedule based on your individual performance profile
  • For each route, Home by Two proposes checkpoints based on the places you plan to visit. Once you reach them, you know how you are doing against your schedule.
  • During the activity, just tell Home by Two when you started the activity and it will tell you if you are on time.
  • Public transport information is retrieved at opentransportdata.swiss.
