Get Home by Two

Plan your outings down to the minute based on your usual speed for the given distance and elevation profile. During the activity, Home by Two tracks wether you are ahead or behind schedule and adapts the estimated time of arrival accordingly.

If you change your plans, you can easily find the best connection back home.

How it works

  • During the activity the times are based on your current travel speed and the remaining distance
  • Public transport information is retrieved at opentransportdata.swiss.
